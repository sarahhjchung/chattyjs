Generate a Full Chatbox

Adding a chatbox to the bottom of your screen is as simple as two calls where you can also customize it however you want. The chatbox will be interactive and the user will be able to open/minimize the chatbox.

After generating a chatbox, you will be able to add questions, links, images, and more to interact with your users and create a fun user experience!

Click on the icon on the bottom right of the screen to see the example. 

                    
    const cb = new Chatbox("#E07A5F", "light")
    cb.makeChatbox()
